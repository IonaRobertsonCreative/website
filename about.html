Iona is an Assistant Stage Manager, Scenic Artist and Prop Maker from Sheffield, England. She is currently in her third year of BA Theatre Design and Production at the University of Wales Trinity Saint David in Carmarthen, Wales. She has a variety of experience in different theatre practices, and she has also undertaken work experience at both Sheffield Theatres and the Hay Festival.

She has just finished working as the Lead Scenic Artist & Set Dresser on Earthquakes in London directed by Ioan Hefin & Keira Sikora.

She is soon to start work as the Assistant Designer & Scenic Artist on both Spring Awakening directed by Luke Hereford & Josephine Wilson and All That I Am directed by Matthew Holquist & Lauren Williams.
